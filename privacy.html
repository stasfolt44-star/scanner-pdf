Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Scanner PDF ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Documents you scan: All scanned documents are stored locally on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your documents.
  • Device information: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve and personalize user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features

Data Storage

All your documents are stored:

  • Locally: On your device in the app's secure container
  • iCloud (optional): In your personal iCloud account if you enable sync

We do not store your documents on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Apple App Store (for purchases and subscriptions)
  • Apple iCloud (for optional document sync)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Documents can be password-protected within the app for additional security.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
